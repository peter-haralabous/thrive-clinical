People today face three fundamental problems with their health information that our personal health experience must solve:

### Problem 1: Scattered, Inaccessible Health Records

**The Reality:**
- Average person has records across 4-7 different healthcare organizations
- Each organization has its own portal with different login credentials

- No way to see the complete picture without manually piecing it together
- When you switch providers, your history doesn't follow you

**The Impact:**
- Repeat the same health history at every new appointment
- Miss important connections between symptoms and conditions
- Duplicate tests because records weren't available
- Feel unprepared and anxious at medical appointments
- Struggle to advocate for yourself without complete information

**Our Solution:**
A single, unified health record that automatically aggregates all your health data â€” from every provider, every document, every system â€” into one complete, organized, and always-accessible view.

- Anxiety from not understanding what your records mean
- Unable to have informed conversations with providers
- Miss important patterns and trends in your own health
